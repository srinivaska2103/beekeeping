Expert Q&A

Sarah Jenkins

"What is the best way to introduce a new queen to a colony that has been queenless for more than a week?"

Master Beekeeper Response

Use a push-in cage over some emerging brood. This allows the queen to pick up the colony scent safely while the young bees immediately accept her. Monitor for 48 hours before full release.
